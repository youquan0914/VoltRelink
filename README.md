**VoltRelink** is a charging management app designed specifically for users who carry two or more phones. It lets your primary device receive notifications when your secondary device is fully charged, helping you avoid leaving a phone plugged in for too long, which can lead to overheating and faster battery degradation over time. Unlike traditional battery apps that only notify on the device itself, VoltRelink focuses on cross‑device battery notifications, so you no longer need to repeatedly wake up and check the other phone’s screen.

Many users leave their backup phone, hotspot device, or download phone plugged in for hours or even overnight, keeping it at 100% almost all the time, which accelerates battery ageing in the long run.

For users who carry multiple phones, constantly checking the secondary device’s battery while studying, working, or gaming is both distracting and inconvenient. VoltRelink aims to solve this very specific but common problem of delayed feedback on secondary device charging status.

Once VoltRelink is installed on two or more Android devices, you can configure one or more devices as “monitor devices” (primary) and others as “monitored devices” (secondary). The app periodically reports the battery level of the secondary device and sends notifications to the primary device when it reaches your configured thresholds.

You can define multiple battery level thresholds, such as getting one alert at 80% and another at 100%, which helps you keep the battery within a healthier range and avoid staying at full charge for too long.

**VoltRelink** 是一款专为「双持党」设计的充电管理应用，帮助用户在副机电量充满时第一时间收到主机通知，从而避免长时间插着电源导致电池过热、续航衰减等问题。 相比只在本机提示电量的传统电池应用，VoltRelink 的特点是可以跨设备通知电量状态，让你不用反复查看另一台手机的屏幕，也能安心充电。

核心痛点

很多人习惯把备用机、热点机、下载机长时间插着充电，常常一插就忘，结果整晚 100% 贴着充，长期下来容易加速电池老化。

市面上大多数电池提醒工具只在单一设备本身发通知，无法在「主力机」上及时知道「另一台手机」已经充满或电量达到某个阈值。

双持用户在学习、工作、打游戏时，很难一直留意副机的电量变化，既麻烦又打断专注。

主要功能
跨设备电量通知

在两台（或多台）Android 设备安装 VoltRelink 后，可以将其中一台,或多台设为「监控设备」（主力机），另一台设为「被监控设备」（副机）。

满电 / 阈值提醒

可以自定义多个提醒阈值，例如 80% 提醒一次、100% 再提醒一次，方便用户控制在更健康的电量区间内充电。
